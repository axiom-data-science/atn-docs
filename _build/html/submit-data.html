How to Submit Data to the IOOS ATN¶

As of July 2018, data can be submitted to the IOOS ATN by uploading it to the Research Workspace, a web-based data management platform developed to facilitate the management, analysis, and publication of scientific data, by following these steps:

  1. Create a Research Workspace account
  2. Log in at https://researchworkspace.com/
  3. Navigate to your project
  4. Upload data files
  5. Create a metadata record describing your data

For assistance submitting data to the IOOS ATN, see the Research Workspace help docs, consult the tutorials below, or contact Axiom Data Science using the information on the Contact Us page.
